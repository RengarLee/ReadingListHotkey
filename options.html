"Add to Reading List" Options

To change the keyboard shortcut for this extension, you need to visit Chrome's extension shortcuts page.

Usage Instructions

Add a page to Reading List: While viewing any webpage, press Ctrl+M (or Command+M on Mac) to quickly add the current page to your Chrome Reading List.

Remove a page from Reading List: While viewing a page that's already in your Reading List, press Ctrl+. (or Command+. on Mac) to remove it.

Mark a page as read: If you have an unread page in your Reading List, press Ctrl+, (or Command+, on Mac) to mark it as read without removing it from the list.

Troubleshooting

If the default shortcuts don't work, they may conflict with other browser shortcuts or extensions. You can customize them by visiting the Chrome extensions shortcuts page linked above.
